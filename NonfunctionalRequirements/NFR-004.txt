REQ-ID: NFR-004
Description: The product shall ensure all ticket(s) available for re-allocation are re-allocated to customers on the waiting list.
Type: Performance
Rationale: There could be empty seats if tickets are not re-allocated after they have been returned.
Fit Criterion: 100% of the returned tickets must be re-allocated to customers on the waiting list when the re-allocation process is run.
Originator: Edna Everage.
Comments: [GG] Req NFR-004 relates to functional requirement REQ-ID: 006.
