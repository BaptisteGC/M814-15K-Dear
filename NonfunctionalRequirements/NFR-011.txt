REQ-ID: NFR-011
Description: Tickets' unique identification numbers should note generate rows.
Type: Security
Rationale: It is important to prevent accidental or intentional misidentification of ticket.
Fit Criterion: Any two tickets’ unique identification numbers should differ at least by two characters.
Originator: Edna Everage
Comments: [Remigiusz Duszynski] Fit criterion should be more abstract, in the current form it unneccessary constrains other solutions like checksum.
