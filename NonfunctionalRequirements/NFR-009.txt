REQ-ID: NFR-009
Description: System will put normal tickets. returned tickets and returned-bought tickets  into separate tables (or separated by a different parameter indentifier),
to ensure the same tickets are always somewhere and process works correctly
Type: Security
Rationale: To prevent edge cases and to prevent duplicately assigned tickets and at the same time for all stakeholders such as event owners to be able to see status of all tickets
Fit Criterion: At all times each of the tickets will be only in one table (or containing only one parameter)  and there will be no duplicates at the same time the volume of all ticket states must be equal to total amount of tickets
Originator: Edna Everage
